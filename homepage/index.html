Zulip Blog Bot

This is our RC Impossible Day 2024 project - a tool that turns Zulip messages directly into blog posts!.

Usage

Send a DM to Blog Bot (HyperTXT) that contains the blog name you'd like to have. This will appear at <username>.hypertxt.io.

Then, to publish something to your site, @-mention Blog Bot (HyperTXT) and that message will be pushed as a post to your site!
